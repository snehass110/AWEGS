About AWEGS

The African Women Entrepreneurs Global Summit (AWEGS) is organized by AFRI ME Trading for the Forum of African Female Entrepreneurs (FAFE). It serves as a permanent bridge between Africa and the UAE for women-led innovation, education, AI advancement, and sustainable economic growth.
